# Customer-Sales-Analysis
### Project Overview :-
This project focuses on analyzing customer sales data to understand revenue trends, customer demographics, purchasing behavior, and retention patterns.
The goal is to provide actionable insights for marketing, sales, and management teams by transforming raw sales data into an interactive, decision-support dashboard.

The dashboard was built using Tableau, leveraging descriptive analytics and customer cohort analysis to track performance over time.

### Explore the interactive dashboard here :-
Customer Sales Analysis Dashboard – [Tableau]()

### Tools Used :-
* Tableau – Data visualization, dashboard creation, and interactive analysis
* CSV Dataset – Sales transaction data used as the data source

### Dataset / Input Data :-
Source: Sales & Customer Transactions

### Key Fields Include:
* Order Date (Month & Year)
* State & Region
* Customer Age & Gender
* Product Category
* Revenue (Total Sales Value)
* Discount Percentage
* Quantity Ordered

### What the Dashboard Shows :-
The Customer Sales & Cohort Analysis Dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales performance and customer behavior.

### 1. Revenue Overview
* Total Revenue: ~$233M
* Month-wise revenue trends highlighting seasonal fluctuations
* High and low performing sales periods across two years

### 2. Geographic Performance
* State-wise revenue map showing sales concentration across regions
* Region-wise revenue contribution (South, Midwest, West, Northeast)
* South region contributes the highest share of overall revenue

### 3. Customer Demographics Analysis
* Age-wise revenue distribution showing strongest spending from 20–60 age groups
* Gender-wise category sales using a butterfly chart to compare male vs female purchasing patterns
* Product category preferences across customer segments

### 4. Discount & Sales Behavior
* Quantity vs Discount Percentage correlation analysis
* Clear positive trend indicating higher discounts generally lead to higher quantities ordered

### 5. Customer Cohort Analysis
* Customers grouped into cohorts based on their first purchase period
* Tracks purchasing behavior and retention trends over subsequent months
* Helps identify:
  * High-value customer cohorts
  * Retention and drop-off patterns
  * Long-term revenue contribution by acquisition period

### Dashboard Features :-
* Interactive category filter applied across all visuals
* Cross-filtering enabled using maps and charts
* Clean KPI display for total revenue
* Executive-friendly layout with clear storytelling

### Results / Output :-
* Key insights derived from the analysis:
* Total revenue of $233M+ generated across regions and categories
* South and Midwest regions are the strongest revenue contributors
* Customers aged 20–60 drive the majority of sales
* Higher discount percentages are associated with increased order quantities
* Cohort analysis reveals that early customer retention significantly impacts long-term revenue

### Conclusion :-
The Customer Sales & Cohort Analysis Dashboard converts raw sales data into meaningful insights that explain where revenue comes from, who the customers are, how they behave, and how retention evolves over time.
This analysis supports data-driven strategies for marketing optimization, pricing decisions, and customer retention planning.
